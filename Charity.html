What The River Trust Does

The Rivers Trust bring together the people, knowledge , data and intelligence to empower communities to take action and be part of the solution; ensuring rivers thrive. Our rivers are an integral part of our communities and The Rivers Trust is leading the movement to protect them. Protecting them from the impacts of pollution from water companies and agriculture as well as the devasting global consequences of climate change.

Why am I supporting The Rivers Trust

As a member of Bradford Amateur Rowing Club, I have spent countless hours rowing on the river Aire and raced on over rivers across the country. Being on the water is very peaceful and I get to enjoy the picturesque views whether it be a cold winter's day or a hot summer's one, you can always find me on the river Aire at Shipley Bradford. However, the impacts of climate change have been felt on our rivers. The increase in frequency of storms across the country has caused excessive flooding. Additionally, the warmer summers and the release of nutrients from agriculture allow algae to bloom, bringing an imbalance to the ecosystem. Not to mention the large amounts of raw sewage being dumped into our waters every day nationwide. These key issues can make our rivers unpleasant and impossible to row on. This creates a natural barrier between nature and our communities that wish to enjoy it as well as having devasting effects on the ecosystem. Hence I wish to support the organisation that is taking action to clean up our rivers and letting them thrive so that the community in generations to come can enjoy them.
